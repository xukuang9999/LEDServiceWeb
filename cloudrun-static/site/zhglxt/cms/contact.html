Enquiry details

What should I send for a commercial LED display enquiry?

Send the site location, LED display type, issue summary or installation goal, preferred timeline, and any photos or constraints that affect access, rigging, power, or maintenance.

Urgent faults

Can you help with urgent onsite LED display faults?

Yes. We can review urgent faults, clarify the likely technical cause, and arrange onsite attendance or remote triage when the job needs fast field service support.

Ongoing support

Do you handle both planned maintenance and new installation work?

Yes. We support new commercial LED display installation, maintenance planning, after-sales service, technical troubleshooting, rental LED display jobs, and stage lighting related onsite support.

What to include

Give us the details that move the job forward.

  • Site or venue location
  • Type of LED display or lighting system involved
  • Whether the request is for installation, maintenance, support, or onsite delivery
  • Preferred dates or urgency
  • Any fault details, photos, or operating constraints

Response style
