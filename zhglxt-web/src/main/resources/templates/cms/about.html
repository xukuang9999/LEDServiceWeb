About Us

A Melbourne-based service team for LED displays, lighting systems, and onsite technical response.

We keep the structure simple: strong technical delivery, practical field execution, and support that clients can rely on when visual systems need to perform across Queensland, Victoria, New South Wales, and Auckland.

Who we are

Technical delivery with real field experience.

LED Service Solutions Pty Ltd is registered in Melbourne, Australia, and focuses on LED display technology, maintenance, technical support, stage lighting delivery, and onsite service.

The company brings together technical engineers and execution specialists who understand both the system design side and the practical demands of working in live, commercial, and event environments.

What clients rely on us for

Clear response, stable execution, and less downtime.

  • LED display installation and commissioning
  • After-sales service and preventive maintenance
  • Technical troubleshooting and recovery support
  • Stage lighting design and installation delivery
  • Onsite attendance for urgent or production-critical needs
